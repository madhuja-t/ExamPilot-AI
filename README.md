# 📚 ExamPilot AI

> AI-powered exam preparation platform that transforms syllabus, notes, and previous year question papers into personalized study material.

## 🚀 Problem Statement

Students often spend hours organizing notes, finding important topics, creating flashcards, and predicting exam questions before every semester. This process is repetitive, time-consuming, and inefficient.

ExamPilot AI automates the entire exam preparation workflow using AI.

---

## ✨ Features

- 📄 Upload syllabus, notes, and previous year question papers
- 🤖 AI-generated chapter summaries
- 📌 Key Topics extraction
- 🔥 Frequently Asked Topics
- 🧠 Mind Map generation
- 📝 AI Quiz generation
- 🎯 Predicted Exam Questions
- 🃏 Flashcard generation
- 📅 Personalized Study Planner
- 📊 Study Analytics Dashboard
- 💬 AI Study Assistant (Ask AI)

## ⚙️ How it Works

1. Upload syllabus, notes, and previous year papers.
2. AI extracts the study material.
3. Generates summaries and key concepts.
4. Predicts important exam questions.
5. Creates flashcards and quizzes.
6. Builds a personalized study plan.
